---
title: How to invite members to my organization
description: Inviting Members to Your Organization in Memorang Hub.
---

To invite members to your organization:

1. **Open Organization Settings:** Click on your organization name and the Settings icon next to it.
2. **Invite Members:** Go to the Members tab, click Invite, enter email addresses, select roles, and send invitations
