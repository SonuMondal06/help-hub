---
title: Can I change the name of my organization?
description: Updating Organization Name in Memorang Hub.
---

To change your organization's name:

1. **Open Organization Settings:** Click on your organization name and the Settings icon next to it.
2. **Update Profile:** Click "Update Profile," change the name, and click Save Changes.
