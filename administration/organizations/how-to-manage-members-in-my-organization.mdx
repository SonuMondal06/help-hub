---
title: How to manage members in my organization
description: Managing Members in Your Organization.
---

To manage members:

1. **Change Role:** Go to the Members tab in the organization settings, select the member, and change their role using the Role dropdown.
2. **Add Member:** Click Invite, enter emails, select roles, and send invitations.
3. **Remove Member:** Click the triple dot next to the member's row and select Remove Member.
