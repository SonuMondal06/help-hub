---
title: How do I switch between projects?
description: Switching Projects in Memorang Hub.
---

To switch between projects:

1. **Select Organization:** Choose your organization.
2. **Choose Project:** Use the organization dashboard to select a project. Use the dropdown menu in the left pane to switch to a different project.
