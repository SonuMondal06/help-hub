---
title: What is a large language model (LLM)?
description: Understanding Large Language Models (LLMs) in Memorang Hub.
---

An **LLM** is an advanced AI model designed to understand and generate human-like text. It can process complex inputs and produce detailed, accurate, and contextually relevant outputs. In EdFlow, agents are powered by LLMs such as GPT-4.
