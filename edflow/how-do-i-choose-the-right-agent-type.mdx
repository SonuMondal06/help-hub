---
title: How do I choose the right agent type?
description: Choosing the right agent type in EdFlow.
---


- **Generator:** For tasks that require new content creation.
- **Evaluator:** For reviewing or improving existing content.
- **Assessor:** For giving detailed feedback or analysis.
- **Revisor:** For editing and refining content.
- **Classifier:** For organizing and categorizing data. Choose the agent type based on the task you need to accomplish. You can always reconfigure it later.
