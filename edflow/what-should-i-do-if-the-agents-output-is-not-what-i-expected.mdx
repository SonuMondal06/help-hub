---
title: What should I do if the agent's output is not what I expected?
description: Handling Unexpected Outputs in EdFlow.
---

Try the following steps:

1. Revisit the **Instructions** and **Outputs** sections to make sure you've provided clear and detailed guidance.
2. Adjust the **Creativity** level or other model settings.
3. Test the agent again in the **Playground** after making adjustments.
