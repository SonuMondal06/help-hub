---
title: How to integrate Memorang with your existing e-commerce platform?
description: Integrating Memorang with BigCommerce.
---

<Note>
  Only BigCommerce integration supported
</Note>

To integrate Memorang with your BigCommerce store, follow these steps:

1. **Prerequisites:**
    - Ensure administrative access to both your BigCommerce store and Memorang account.
    - Obtain the External Plan ID and Variant ID from your BigCommerce store.
2. **Integration Steps:**
    - **Navigate to the E-commerce Integration Page:** Go to Pages/Ecommerce in Memorang.
    - **Create a New Integration Record:** Select Product and Plan, then input the External Plan ID and Variant ID from BigCommerce.
    - **Save the Integration:** Click Create to finalize the integration.
