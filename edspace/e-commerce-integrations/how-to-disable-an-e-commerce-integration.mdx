---
title: How to disable an e-commerce integration?
description: Disabling E-commerce Integrations in Memorang Hub.
---

To disable an e-commerce integration in Memorang Hub:

1. **Locate the Integration Record:** Go to the E-commerce Integration Page and find the record.
2. **Toggle isActive Field:** Switch the isActive field off.
3. **Confirm Deactivation:** Ensure the record status indicates it is no longer active.
