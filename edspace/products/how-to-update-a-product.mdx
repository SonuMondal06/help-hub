---
title: How to update a product?
description: Updating Product Details in Memorang Hub.
---

To keep your product information current, follow these steps to update a product in Memorang Hub:

1. **Locate the Product:** Navigate to the products page and find the product you want to update.
2. **Edit the Product Details:** Click on the product's row to open the editing interface. Update the SKU, product name, description, or exam as needed.
3. **Save Changes:** Ensure all updates are correct and click Save to apply the changes.
