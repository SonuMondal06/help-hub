---
title: What are products?
description: Understanding the Basics of Products in Memorang Hub.
---


In Memorang Hub, products represent the educational content packages 
available for purchase through your integrated e-commerce store. These 
products can include study packs, exam bundles, practice tests, and 
more.

Each product is identified by a specific SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) 
and can be associated with various plans and exams.

Proper management of these products is crucial for ensuring a smooth purchase and access 
experience for your customers. This guide provides instructions on 
managing products within Memorang Hub, covering the creation, updating, 
and deletion of products to help you efficiently handle your offerings.
