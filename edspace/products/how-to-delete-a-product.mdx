---
title: How to delete a product?
description: Removing Products from Memorang Hub.
---

Deleting a product from Memorang Hub is a simple process. Here's how you can do it:

1. **Locate the Product:** Navigate to the products page and find the product you wish to delete.
2. **Delete the Product:** Select the checkbox next to the product's row. Click the Delete button at the top of the product list.
3. **Confirm Deletion:** Confirm the deletion action in the prompt to permanently remove the product from the system.
