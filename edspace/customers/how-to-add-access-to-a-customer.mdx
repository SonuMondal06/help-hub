---
title: How to add access to a customer?
description: Granting Product Access to Customers in Memorang Hub.
---

To add access to a customer in Memorang:

1. **Locate the Customer:** Go to the customers page and select the customer.
2. **Open Access Management:** Scroll down to the Access section in the detailed view. Click the + button to add new access.
3. **Select Product or Content:** Choose the product or content to grant access to and click Add to confirm.
