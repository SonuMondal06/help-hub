---
title: How to update a customer's email address?
description: Updating Customer Email Addresses in Memorang Hub.
---

To update a customer's email address:

1. **Locate the Customer:** Go to the customers page and find the customer. Use the search functionality to locate them quickly.
2. **Edit Email:** Click on the customer's row to open the detailed view, then click on the email field to edit it.
3. **Update and Save:** Enter the new email address and click Save to update.
