---
title: How can I view customer information?
description: Viewing Customer Details in Memorang Hub.
---

To view customer information in Memorang Hub:

1. **Navigate to the Customers Page:** Go to EdSpace -> Customers on the left-hand sidebar.
2. **Select a Customer:** Click on the row of the customer you want to view. Use the search bar to find customers by name, email, or other identifiers.
3. **View Customer Details:** The detailed view will display email, username, UUID, joined date, analytics links, and access to products or content.
