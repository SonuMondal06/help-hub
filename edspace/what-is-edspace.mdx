---
title: What is EdSpace?
description: A back-office tool for partners.
---

Edspace is a back-office tool for partners that offers a comprehensive suite of
features including CMS, CRM, integrations, and E-Commerce. It is 
specifically designed to help partners build, grow, and manage every 
aspect of their Memorang-powered services. With Edspace, partners can 
easily create and customize their own AI-driven learning applications, 
seamlessly integrate with third-party tools, and efficiently manage 
their content and services. Whether it's managing customer 
relationships, optimizing content workflows, or driving E-Commerce 
growth, Edspace provides the necessary tools and capabilities to 
streamline operations and maximize success.


## Features of EdSpace

- **CMS:** Edspace offers a powerful Content
Management System (CMS) that allows partners to easily create and manage their own AI-driven learning applications. This feature enables
partners to customize their applications according to their specific
needs.
- **CRM:** Edspace includes a Customer
Relationship Management (CRM) tool that helps partners effectively
manage their customer relationships. With this feature, partners can
track customer interactions, manage leads, and improve customer
satisfaction.
- **Integrations:** Edspace seamlessly
integrates with third-party tools, allowing partners to leverage
existing systems and workflows. This feature enables partners to
streamline their operations and enhance productivity.
- **E-Commerce:** Edspace provides
E-Commerce capabilities, empowering partners to drive growth and
monetize their services. With this feature, partners can easily sell
their learning applications, courses, or other digital products.

These features of Edspace help partners build, grow, and manage their Memorang-powered services efficiently and effectively.
